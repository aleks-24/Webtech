Dutch Fried Chicken

Craving some delicious Dutch Chicken? Maybe you’re in the mood for some tasty fries? No matter what kind of meal you have in mind, Dutch Fried Chicken is ready to prepare it for you.

Since 1973, Dutch Fried Chicken has been the go-to restaurant for residents of the Netherlands. Our restaurant serves breakfast all day, in addition to wholesome and flavorful dining options for lunch and dinner. From burgers to salads, sandwiches to buckets, you’ll find all kinds of hearty meals prepared fresh at Dutch Fried Chicken. Our restaurant also has a full bakery with delicious baked goods and other treats, including our famous cheesy bread. Sounds delicious, right?

We’re here to serve you the best food around, whenever you’re looking for a great Dutch restaurant in your neighbourhood.
